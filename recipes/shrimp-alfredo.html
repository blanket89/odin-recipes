Shrimp Alfredo

Finished product:

Warm, creamy pasta... with shrimp :D

Short disclaimer:

Italian chef not included, you must make on your own. This is like a little pick-me-up for when you can't afford to eat out but want to bring the vibes to the crib.

Ingredients

  • Eight (8) ounces fettuccine or your favourite pasta
  • One (1) tablespoon butter
  • One (1) pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • One (1) tablespoon cajun seasoning
  • Two (2) cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine or chicken broth
  • One (1) cup heavy/whipping cream
  • One (1) cup parmigiano reggiano (parmesan), grated
  • 1/2 tablespoon cajun seasoning or to taste
  • 1/4 cup green onion, sliced (optional)

Steps

  1. Start cooking the pasta.
  2. Melt the butter in a pan over medium-high heat, toss the shrimp in the cajun seasoning, add to pan and cook, about 2-3 minutes per side, and set aside.
  3. Add the garlic to the pan and saute until fragrant, about a 30 seconds.
  4. Deglaze the pan with the wine, add the heavy cream, parmesan and cajun seasoning and cook until it thickens a bit, about 3-5 minutes.
  5. Toss the pasta in with the alfredo sauce along with the shrimp and serve garnished with green onions.

This is for fun. I haven't verified the authenticity of the recipe nor is it the one I would use.
