Apple Pie

Finished product:

Warm, golden apple pie

Short disclaimer:

This award winning apple pie recipe makes a pie so good, your grandma will get knocked straight outta her socks. Please bake with caution.

-- Awards being won not guarenteed --

Ingredients

  • 9-inch pie crust, fully defrosted if recently frozen
  • One (1) stick of butter
  • Three (3) Tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • One (1) tsp ground cinnamon
  • One (1) tsp vanilla extract
  • Five (5) cups peeled and sliced Granny Smith apple

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Prepare bottom shell pie crust, cover and refrigerate.
  3. Melt butter in a skillet or large saucepan over medium heat.
  4. Whisk in flour, add water, sugars, cinnamon and vanilla, stir to combine.
  5. Bring to a boil, reduce temperature to a simmer. Add the apples, and let simmer 5 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. In a small saucepan over medium-low heat, add 1/8 cup of the sauce from the other pan, plus the remaining tablespoon of butter, allow butter to melt, stir to combine, remove from heat, and set aside.
  7. Fill the pie crust with the apple mixture.
  8. Cover with top pie crust, crimping edges to hold together, cut slits in top to vent.
  9. Evenly spread/brush the glaze on the top pie crust.
  10. Bake for about 45-50 minutes or until golden brown.
